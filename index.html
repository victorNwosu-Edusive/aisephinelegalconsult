WHAT WE SPECIALIZE IN

Our Practice areas

Dispute Resolution

At Aisephine Legal Consult, we provide strategic, result-driven dispute resolution services tailored to protect our clients’ interests. Whether you are facing commercial disagreements, contractual issues, business conflicts, or personal disputes, we prioritize efficient solutions that save time, cost, and stress. Our approach combines mediation, negotiation, and litigation when necessary, ensuring that every matter is handled with professionalism, clarity, and a commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

Family Matters

We understand that family-related legal issues require not only legal expertise but also compassion and sensitivity. Our firm handles a wide range of family matters including marital disputes, custody arrangements, guardianship, divorce processes, inheritance matters, and domestic agreements. We guide clients through emotionally challenging situations with respect and confidentiality, while ensuring that their rights and interests are fully protected throughout the process.

Incorporation Services

Aisephine Legal Consult offers complete legal support for business formation and corporate structuring. Whether you are starting a small business, registering an NGO, or formalizing a growing enterprise, we make the incorporation process seamless. Our services include company registration, regulatory compliance, drafting of corporate documents, board resolutions, business name registration, restructuring, and CAC documentation. We ensure your business meets all statutory requirements while positioning you for stability and long-term growth.

Property Consultancy

We provide comprehensive legal guidance on property acquisition, leasing, real estate transactions, and land matters. From conducting due diligence to preparing property agreements and verifying titles, we help clients avoid fraud and legal pitfalls. Our team ensures that every transaction is transparent, legally compliant, and secure. Whether you are buying, selling, leasing, or resolving land disputes, we offer expert support to safeguard your investment and ensure peace of mind.
